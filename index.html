The Degree Explained

What Is BSIT Exactly?

If you're wondering whether IT is the right path, here's everything you need to know.

What You Study

BSIT covers the full spectrum of information technology — programming, networks, databases, web development, cybersecurity, data analytics, and systems administration. You graduate as a versatile tech professional, not locked into one lane.

Program Duration

The BSIT program runs for 4 years (8 semesters). The final year includes an on-the-job training (OJT) component and a capstone project, ensuring you graduate with real industry experience already on your résumé.

Career Paths

Graduates pursue roles as software developers, network engineers, cybersecurity analysts, database administrators, IT support specialists, systems analysts, cloud engineers, and more — both locally and abroad.

Industry Demand

The Philippines is one of Southeast Asia's fastest-growing tech talent hubs. BPO, fintech, e-commerce, and government digitization are all driving unprecedented demand for BSIT graduates with practical skills.

Certifications You Can Earn

Beyond your diploma, STI Calamba prepares you for globally recognized credentials: Cisco CCNA, CompTIA A+, CompTIA Security+, AWS Cloud Practitioner, Microsoft Azure Fundamentals, and Oracle Database certifications.

Why Start Now

Technology is not a sector — it is every sector. Whether you aim to work in healthcare, finance, entertainment, or government, IT skills make you indispensable. There has never been a better time to start.
